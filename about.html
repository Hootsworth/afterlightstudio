Our Mission

To create technology that is simple, intuitive, and respects your attention.

User Experience is Key

Afterlight Studio was founded on a simple belief: in a world of endless notifications, we need software that feels like a quiet room. We believe software should be an extension of your mind, not a distraction. Our design process starts and ends with the user. Everything else is secondary.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide every decision.

Privacy First

Your data is yours. Period. We build our apps to be private by default. That means no trackers, no ads, and no data mining. We believe privacy is a fundamental human right.

Thoughtful Design

We believe software should be beautiful, intuitive, and calm. We obsess over details to create distraction-free experiences that respect your time and attention.

Wellbeing Focused

Our goal is to create tools that genuinely improve your life. We build technology that fosters mental clarity, encourages healthy habits, and supports your peace of mind.

The Journey So Far

Start of June

The first lines of code for Drift are written, born from a desire for a simple, private journaling space.

End of July

After weeks of refinement, Drift is published on the Google Play Store for the public to download.

8th August

The Drift 2.0 update is pushed, introducing a refreshed design and new features based on early user feedback.

End of September

Teehee closed testing begins with a select group of users.

October 2025

Teehee, our smart reminders app, enters open testing on the Google Play Store.
